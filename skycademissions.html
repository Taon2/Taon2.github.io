SkycadeMissions

SkycadeMissions is a custom plugin made for Skycade LTD created by myself.
The plugin gives players daily missions to complete for beneficial rewards.

Plugin Features

Daily Missions reset every 24 hours, and are the same for all players. The plugin is infinitely expandable when a new mission type is required.

Mission Types contain the following existing types:

- Inventory - Tracks the amount of specific items in an inventory.  Used Globally.

- Land - Tracks placed blocks or entities around the player.  Used Globally.

- Damage - Tracks the amount of damage dealt to a specific mob.  Used Globally.

- Kills - Tracks the amount of kills on a specific mob.  Used Globally.

- Mining - Tracks the amount of a specific type of block broken. Used Globally.

- Shop - Tracks the amount of items sold to the shop. Used Globally.

- Enchant - Tracks the amount of enchanted books used. Used for Prisons.

- Generate - Tracks the amount of generated enchanted books. Used for Prisons.

- Swindle - Tracks the amount of money gained from the Swindle enchant. Used for Prisons.

- SnowballGun - Tracks hitting players with the Snowball Gun enchant. Used Globally.

- Fishing - Tracks the item received from fishing. Used for Prisons.

- Level - Tracks the player's XP level. Used for Prisons.

- SpecialAbility - Tracks how many times a kit's special ability is used. Used for KitPvP.

- KitKill - Tracks how many kills a player gets in a specific kit. Used for KitPvP.

- KillStreak - Tracks the highest killstreak gained during the 24 hour period. Used for KitPvP.

- CoinReward - Tracks a coin currency increase. Used for KitPvP.

- IslandLevel - Tracks an increase in Skyblock Island Level. Used for Skyblock.

- Taming - Tracks the taming of a specified animal type. Used for SMP.


Mission Rewards are configurable by the level specification of the mission, and relies on a console command performed upon completion. Several rewards can be listed, and the plugin will randomly choose one of the potential rewards to be granted.

The active Missions GUI. 3 missions every 24 hours that are the same for all players. 
The Rewards GUI. This displays the potential rewards for each level of mission.
